ICT Procurements for 2021-2022

This is with reference to the Circular Nos PS/GPA/Circular/01/2020 dated and 13th January 2020 and PS/SP/Circular/05/2021 dated 02nd March 2021 (the “Circulars”) issued by the Presidential Secretariat conveying the direction of H.E. the President regarding the above subject.

The Circulars mentioned at the outset indicate a report to be submitted by Government institutions to the Presidential Secretariat with information pertaining to each of their respective ICT initiatives. The Circulars further direct ICTA to assess such ICT initiatives with the objective of ensuring, amongst other things, interoperability of systems, avoiding duplication and sharing of experiences of one organization with another.

Therefore, we would request and appreciate if the related information as requested for by the Circulars be communicated to ICTA, in order for ICTA to prepare a comprehensive report to the Secretary to His Excellency, President of Sri Lanka. In furtherance of this process and for your convenience, ICTA has developed this online form which would assist you to provide the requested information, and enable ICTA to compile a complete list of digital solutions being implemented by Government institutions as a whole.
